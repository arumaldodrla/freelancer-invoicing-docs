# Privacy, GDPR, and DPIA

This document outlines the privacy and data protection measures for the Freelancer Invoicing & Gig Income Tracking application, with a focus on GDPR compliance and the Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA).

## 1. Data Inventory

| Data Category | Purpose | Lawful Basis | Retention Period |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| User Profile Data | To create and manage user accounts | Contract | For the duration of the user's account |
| Income & Expense Data | To provide the core features of the application | Contract | For the duration of the user's account |
| Tax Summary Data | To provide tax estimation features | Contract | For the duration of the user's account |
| Subscription & Billing Data | To manage user subscriptions | Contract | For the duration of the user's account |
| Audit Log Data | For security and compliance purposes | Legitimate Interest | 2-5 years |

## 2. Consent Flows

- **Marketing Communications:** Users will be required to provide explicit consent to receive marketing communications.
- **Receipt Scanning:** Users will be required to provide explicit consent to use the receipt scanning feature, which involves sharing data with an AI provider.

## 3. Data Subject Rights

- **Right of Access:** Users will be able to export their data in a structured format (JSON or CSV).
- **Right to Rectification:** Users will be able to edit their profile and manually entered data.
- **Right to Erasure:** Users will be able to delete their account and all associated data.
- **Right to Restriction/Objection/Portability:** Users will have the ability to opt-out of certain analytics and export their data.

## 4. Subprocessors

A list of all subprocessors and their data center regions will be maintained and made available to users. All subprocessors will be required to sign a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) and, where applicable, Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

## 5. Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)

A DPIA will be conducted for all major new features that involve the processing of personal data. The DPIA will assess the risks to data subjects and identify measures to mitigate those risks.

## 6. AI Support Data Usage

The AI support agent will only have access to the data necessary to answer a user's question. All personally identifiable information (PII) will be minimized in prompts, and users will be required to provide consent for their data to be used by the AI support agent.
